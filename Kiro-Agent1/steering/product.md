# Product Overview

## TodoStock SA - Wholesale Cleaning Supplies Distributor

TodoStock SA is a landing page for a wholesale distributor specializing in cleaning and hygiene products. The company targets B2B clients including resellers, supermarkets, hotels, gyms, hardware stores, and other commercial entities across Argentina.

### Core Value Proposition
- Wholesale pricing with volume discounts
- Catalog of 500+ cleaning and hygiene products
- Own logistics fleet with national coverage
- 15+ years in the market serving 3,000+ active clients
- Custom credit lines for regular customers
- White label product manufacturing

### Target Audience
Commercial buyers, distributors, business owners seeking bulk cleaning supplies at competitive prices with reliable delivery.

### Brand Identity
Professional yet approachable wholesale distributor with strong emphasis on service quality, logistics efficiency, and pricing transparency.
